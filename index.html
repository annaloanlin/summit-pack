What it does
Everything you need before the mountain
AI weight search

Type a product name like "MSR Hubba Hubba 2" and the AI searches manufacturer specs online to fill in the weight automatically.

Photo identification

Snap or upload a photo of any piece of gear and the AI identifies it, categorises it, and looks up its weight — no typing needed.

9 categories

Shelter, sleep, clothing, food, navigation, safety, tools, hygiene, and other — your pack weight broken down by what matters most.

Trip planning

Create named trips with a date, pick which gear you're bringing from your full kit, and see the trip total weight at a glance.

CSV export

Download any trip as a spreadsheet — item name, category, weight per unit, quantity, and total. Share it, print it, archive it.

kg or lb

Switch between kilograms and pounds anytime — your gear weights instantly recalculate across every view and export.
